Questions Answered

What is a Naledi avatar?

Naledi is an AI business assistant with a name, face, and personality. She represents your brand on WhatsApp — answering messages, booking appointments, and building relationships with your clients.

Can I customise her?

Yes. Choose Receptionist or Sales personality out of the box, or go fully custom with your own brand face, tone, and colours. Custom avatars start at contact for pricing.

How fast can I get started?

Standard avatars go live in 48 hours. Custom avatars take 1-2 weeks depending on complexity.

Do I need technical skills?

No. We handle everything. You just connect your WhatsApp number. We train Naledi on your business, and she goes live.
