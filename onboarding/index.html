Step-by-Step Process

1
BP / Medical Rep

Doctor Says Yes

Doctor agrees to sign up. BP collects: practice name, doctor's name, practice WhatsApp number, email address. BP sends this to Orion via WhatsApp.

2
Orion + Doctor

Payment

Orion sends the doctor a payment link (PayFast, Yoco, Stripe, PayPal). Doctor pays setup fee + first month (or chooses contract special). Payment is confirmed automatically via PayFast ITN webhook.

3
Orion + Doctor

Welcome & Data Collection

Naledi sends a welcome message to the doctor's WhatsApp. She collects via chat:
- Practice name and address
- Operating hours
- Services offered
- Booking rules (same-day? walk-ins? how far ahead?)
- Pricing for common services
- Insurance / medical aids accepted
- Any FAQs patients ask
Doctor can voice-note all of this. Naledi transcribes and organizes it.

4
Orion handles

WABA Setup (WhatsApp Business API)

If doctor needs a new WABA number: Orion registers it via Meta (R4,690 once-off). If doctor has their own WABA: we connect it for free. Takes 2-6 hours depending on Meta approval.

5
Naledi learns

Training Session

Orion feeds all collected info into Naledi's knowledge base. Naledi learns the practice's hours, services, pricing, booking rules, and FAQs. A quick test session runs: Orion sends sample patient messages to verify Naledi responds correctly.

6
Doctor approves

Testing & Approval

Doctor sends test messages to their own WhatsApp. Naledi responds. Doctor sees how it works, requests any changes. Orion tweaks the knowledge base if needed.

7
Live

Go-Live

Naledi is live on the doctor's WhatsApp. Real patients start messaging. Naledi handles enquiries, books appointments, sends reminders. Doctor gets a daily summary. Ongoing support via WhatsApp.

What the BP / Medical Rep Does

  • Identify interested doctors
  • Introduce ORION PRO (show features page + this onboarding page)
  • Let Naledi demo herself on the doctor's phone
  • Collect practice name, WhatsApp number, email from the doctor
  • Send the lead to Orion via WhatsApp
  • That's it. Orion handles the rest.

What the Doctor Does

  • Say yes to the BP
  • Pay the setup fee via payment link
  • Answer Naledi's questions about their practice (voice notes allowed)
  • Review and approve the test responses
  • Start receiving patient messages handled by Naledi
  • Done. Takes 30 minutes of their time total.

What Orion Does

  • Handle all Meta/WABA registration
  • Train Naledi on the practice
  • Run the test session
  • Go-live deployment
  • Ongoing support and monitoring
  • Monthly reports to the doctor
  • Pay commission to the BP

Payment Flow

Day 1: Doctor pays R4,690 setup + R6,999 first month (special) = R11,689 total via PayFast link.
Monthly thereafter: R6,999/month (first 3 months at special rate) or R8,999/month (month-to-month).
BP commission: 10-15% of monthly subscription for the lifetime of the client.
Payment methods: PayFast, Yoco, Stripe, PayPal.
