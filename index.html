About

The person behind Orion Ventures

I'm Graham Schubach. 26 years in hospitality — from the kitchens of JW Marriott Dubai to running entertainment at Durban's best venues. In November 2025, I founded Orion Ventures to do something different.

I'm a karaoke host, a DJ, a quiz master, and a software developer. Not because I couldn't decide — but because I believe the best events blend showmanship with technology.

I built SupaTraxx SupaBook, an AI-powered karaoke platform with 667,000+ songs, real-time song requests from any phone, and automated queue management. My events are powered by the latest tech, driven by data, and delivered with soul.

Based in Durban, South Africa — available nationwide.
