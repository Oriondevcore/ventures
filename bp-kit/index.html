FAQ

How do I get paid?
Commission is calculated monthly based on active paid subscriptions from doctors you referred. Paid via EFT before the 7th of each month.
What if a doctor cancels?
You stop earning on that specific referral. Cancel rates are low — Naledi retention is strong because practices rely on her daily.
How do I track my referrals?
Every partner gets a unique referral link. We track signups, payments, and your commission. You get a monthly statement.
Can I do this part-time?
Yes. Most partners do this alongside their existing medical rep routes. One conversation per practice is often enough.
