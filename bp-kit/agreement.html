Business Partner Agreement

ORION WhatsApp PRO — Independent Contractor Partnership

Date: ________________
Parties: Orion Ventures (CK [number]) and ________________
Product: ORION WhatsApp PRO (R2,690/mo + R4,690 once-off setup)

This Agreement sets out the terms under which the Partner refers clients to Orion Ventures for ORION WhatsApp PRO. Both parties agree to the following:

1. Relationship

Partner is an independent contractor, not an employee, agent, joint venturer, or franchisee of Orion. No salary, benefits, UIF, PAYE, or working hours are provided or implied. Partner operates their own business and refers clients to Orion under their own name and brand.

Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment relationship. Partner is solely responsible for their own tax, VAT, and regulatory compliance.

2. Partner Starter Kit

Partner purchases a starter kit for R500 once-off, which includes:

  • Certified partner status (1 year, renewable annually at no additional cost)
  • Digital sales manual with scripts, objection handling, and demo flow
  • Access to partner-only demo environment
  • Listing on Orion website as authorized partner
  • Partner badge for WhatsApp profile and business cards
  • Ongoing support from the Orion team

3. Territory & Exclusivity

Exclusive Territory: Partner has exclusive rights to medical practices in KwaZulu-Natal. No other partner will be assigned medical practices in this territory. Orion retains the right to sell directly to any client in any territory.

If Partner wishes to operate outside KwaZulu-Natal, they may do so on a non-exclusive basis with no territory protection. Partner must notify Orion before operating outside their exclusive territory.

4. Commission Structure

Commission is paid on a per-client basis as follows:

Item Client Price Partner Commission Frequency
Monthly subscription R2,690 R300/month Monthly
Meta setup fee R4,690 R500 once-off Once per client

Payment terms:

  • Commission paid monthly within 7 days of Orion receiving the client's payment
  • Partner receives commission for as long as the client remains subscribed and Orion receives payment
  • No cap on total commission — Partner may refer unlimited clients
  • Orion will provide a monthly statement showing active clients and commission earned

5. Partner Obligations

  • Present ORION WhatsApp PRO honestly and accurately. Do not make promises Orion has not authorized.
  • Forward interested clients to Orion for formal onboarding. Partner may not collect payments from clients.
  • Not misrepresent pricing, capabilities, or delivery timelines.
  • Not sell competing WhatsApp automation or AI receptionist products during this Agreement.
  • Maintain confidentiality of Orion's pricing, processes, and client information.
  • Not use Orion branding in ways that imply employment, endorsement, or partnership beyond this Agreement.

6. Orion Obligations

  • Handle all technical setup: WhatsApp Business API registration, Naledi configuration, training, and go-live.
  • Provide ongoing support to clients throughout their subscription.
  • Pay commissions accurately and on time.
  • Keep Partner informed of product changes, pricing updates, and marketing materials.
  • Provide Partner with marketing materials and sales support.

7. Client Onboarding Process

The following process governs all client referrals:

  • Partner identifies an interested client and introduces ORION WhatsApp PRO
  • Partner refers the client to oriondevcore.com/doctors or sends their WhatsApp number to Orion
  • Orion handles: setup call, WABA registration (if needed), Naledi training, configuration, and go-live
  • Partner is entitled to commission from the date the client's first payment is received by Orion
  • Partner may check on client satisfaction but must direct all support requests to Orion

8. Term & Termination

  • Initial term: 12 months from the date of this Agreement
  • Renewal: Auto-renews for successive 12-month periods unless either party gives 30 days written notice before the end of the current term
  • Termination for breach: Either party may terminate immediately by written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to remedy the breach within 14 days of written notice
  • Effect of termination: Partner continues to receive commission on existing active clients for as long as those clients remain subscribed and Orion receives payment. No commission is payable on new clients referred after termination.

9. Non-Compete

During this Agreement and for 6 months after termination, Partner may not:

  • Promote, sell, or represent a competing WhatsApp automation, AI receptionist, or customer communication product
  • Solicit or attempt to solicit any Orion client for competing services
  • Use Orion's confidential information (including pricing, client lists, processes) for any purpose outside this Agreement

This clause does not prevent Partner from continuing their existing medical sales business for non-competing products.

10. Marketing & Branding

  • Orion provides: digital sales sheet, referral link, demo access, social media graphics
  • Partner may use Orion branding as specified in the brand guide provided at onboarding
  • Partner must not modify Orion logos, trademarks, or marketing materials without written approval
  • All marketing materials must be pre-approved by Orion if they go beyond the provided templates

11. Limitation of Liability

Orion's total liability under this Agreement is limited to the commission amounts unpaid in the preceding 3 months. In no event is Orion liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages, including lost profits or business interruption.

12. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa. Any disputes shall be submitted to the mediation or arbitration in accordance with the rules of AFSA (Arbitration Foundation of Southern Africa), with proceedings held in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal.

13. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. Any modifications must be in writing and signed by both parties.
