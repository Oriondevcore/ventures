Commission is paid on **profit**, not total revenue:

| Item | Price | Est. Cost | Profit | Partner Share (33%) |
|------|-------|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| Monthly subscription | R2,690 | R[cost] | R[profit] | R[amount]/month |

- Commission paid monthly, within 7 days of Orion receiving client payment

- No cap on total commission

## 5. Partner Obligations

- Present ORION WhatsApp PRO honestly and accurately
- Forward interested clients to Orion for onboarding
- Not misrepresent pricing or capabilities
- Not sell competing products during this agreement
- Maintain client confidentiality

## 6. Orion Obligations

- Handle all technical setup (WhatsApp API, Naledi training, go-live)
- Provide ongoing support to clients
- Pay commissions on time
- Keep Partner informed of product changes

## 7. Client Onboarding Flow

1. Partner identifies interested doctor
2. Partner introduces concept (using provided scripts/sales sheet)
3. Doctor visits `oriondevcore.com/doctors` and chats with Naledi demo
4. If interested, Partner sends doctor's WhatsApp number to Orion
5. Orion handles: setup call, WABA registration, Naledi training, go-live
6. Partner receives commission for the lifetime of the client

## 8. Term & Termination

- Initial term: 12 months
- Auto-renewed unless either party gives 30 days notice
- Either party may terminate immediately for material breach
- Commission continues for existing clients after termination

## 9. Non-Compete

During this agreement and for 6 months after, Partner may not:
- Promote a competing WhatsApp automation product
- Solicit Orion clients for competing services
